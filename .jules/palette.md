
## 2025-02-15 - Analysis Wizard Accessibility and Polish
**Learning:** Vercel Web Interface Guidelines emphasize strict accessibility and micro-interactions, such as `aria-label` for icon buttons, `htmlFor`/`id` bindings for `<label>` and `<select>`, proper non-breaking spaces for units (`&nbsp;MB`), and correct typography (`…` instead of `...`). Additionally, custom focus indicators (`focus-visible:ring-*`) are strictly required.
**Action:** Applied these improvements to `src/features/upload/components/AnalysisWizard.tsx`.
